## How to log in

1. Go to [rank.pagelyft.studio/login](https://rank.pagelyft.studio/login).
2. Enter the email address and password your admin provided.
3. Click **Sign In**.

You'll be redirected to the main dashboard after a successful login.

## Authentication details

**Email and password only.** RankLyft does not support Google, GitHub, or any other OAuth provider. You must use the email and password combination your admin set up for you.

**No self-service signup.** There is no "Create an account" link. Accounts are provisioned by PageLyft staff. If you need access, contact your PageLyft account manager.

**Sessions auto-refresh.** RankLyft uses Supabase session tokens that refresh automatically in the background. You stay logged in across browser sessions without needing to re-enter your credentials.

**Session expiry.** If your session expires (for example, after an extended period of inactivity), RankLyft redirects you to `/login` automatically. After logging in again, you're returned to your original destination.

## Password issues

RankLyft does not have a self-service password reset flow. If you've forgotten your password or need it changed, contact your PageLyft admin directly. They can reset your credentials through the Supabase dashboard.

<Warning>
  Do not share your login credentials with other team members. If multiple people at your agency need access, ask your admin to create separate accounts for each person.
</Warning>
